Final Project: "Asteroid Attack"

Asteroid Attack is a game where you control a spaceship with your mouse cursor to fly through
outer space. The objective is to survive as long as you can by dodging the asteroids, and collecting
gems. If you get hit by an asteroid, the game ends. The amount of gems you collect is your score.
Every 10 gems you collect, the speed of the asteroids increases.
